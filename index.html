About Me

I am currently enrolled in the University of Denver's Full Stack Coding Bootcamp. My journey here started after eight years in customer service left me feeling unfulfilled. While I was confident in my soft skills, I realized that I had very limited technical skills. I decided to explore the tech industry due to its high growth rate. Initially, I pursued a Google Certification for UX design. I found it interesting but not my true passion. However, the certification program introduced me to the role of Software Developers, which I decided to explore. Through FreeCodeCamp's certification, I discovered my love for coding - a blend of creativity, problem-solving, and attention to detail that resonated with me. With the guidance of a mentor, I gained insight into what a Software Developer role would be like, solidifying my path forward. I am excited to learn a lot and cannot wait for what the future holds!
